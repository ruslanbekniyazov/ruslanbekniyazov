Microsoft Explore

Amongst the experiences that I consider the most significant to my learning, my summer internship at Microsoft ranks very high. My first exposure to the industry has been a smooth experience, thanks to the great culture and the team that I was a part of. Looking back, it surprises me how much I was able to learn in 12 weeks, and I am looking forward to my Software Engineering internship in the summer of this year!

In those 12 weeks, our team of 3 was tasked with developing a gamified, full-stack training solution aimed to test the security knowledge of Microsoft employees. We were able to accomplish that using ASP.NET, utilizing the MVC design pattern, Unity engine, WebGL, Azure, and other tools throughout the development process. We implemented leaderboards to track employee progress and incentivize learning.

During the internship, I expereinced both the Product Management and Software Engineering disciplines. I gained the skills that I will definitely use going forward, and I expanded my network along the way.

StartupUW

Seattle is a hub to a huge amount of talent, and I believe that University of Washington should become a center of innovation and startups in the U.S. That is why I joined StartupUW, the biggest entrepreneurship club at UW. Together, starting with a small team of about 8 people, we were able to reorganize the club and split it into intiatives, and me, I was in charge of Startup-a-thon.

Startup-a-thon was an early-stage startup competion, and with hours of work being put into development, planning, and marketing, we were able to host a grandiose event with over 100 participants. From renowned speakers to useful startup workshops, this event was a success.

Becoming more and more indulged in the operations of the club, I became the Executive VP of the club, until later I progressed to the position of a Co-President. With dedicaiton, we are striving towards making StartupUW the central node for student startups that start at UW Seattle. Click here to check out the club, and the website we built.
